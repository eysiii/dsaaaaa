✦ A Fun Expedition into
Data Structures and Algorithms ✦
Hey fellow learners! Welcome to "GroupName," where we proudly unveil our completed projects, each a testament to our journey mastering data structures and algorithms. Join us in celebrating the exciting worlds of coding challenges and problem-solving!
Sneak Peek:
  • Search Algorithms Comparison
    Step into our search algorithm project, where we've implemented and meticulously compared the performance of various search algorithms on different datasets. Witness the efficiency of algorithms like binary search, linear search, and more, unraveling the strengths and weaknesses of each in different scenarios.
  • Infix to Postfix with the Stack
    Brace yourselves for our stack project, where we've harnessed the Shunting Yard algorithm's magic to convert infix expressions to postfix. Our unique twist on this classic algorithm adds a flair to expression evaluation, showcasing the elegance of stack manipulation.
  • Queue and Deque with Linked Lists
    Dive into the world of queues and deques in our project where we've implemented these dynamic structures using linked lists. Discover the seamless operations of enqueue, dequeue, push front, pop front, push back, and pop back, making these data structures versatile companions in our coding adventures.
  • Hash Table
    Immerse yourself in the realm of hash tables, where we've implemented the insertion and deletion of 10-letter words with precision. Explore the intricacies of our hash function and collision resolution strategy, witnessing how these elements come together to create an efficient and dynamic data storage solution.
